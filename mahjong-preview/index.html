Featured samples

Three design directions under review

These are original design drafts. Real sample photos and inserted-card transparency checks can be added after the next sample shoot.

Midnight Garden

A deep blue garden series with layered florals, birds, crane, koi, and peacock artwork. This direction feels the most giftable and has the strongest background depth for reducing see-through compared with lighter patterns.

  • Full-background artwork
  • Strongest premium gift direction
  • Designed for large-print American Mahjong cards

Blush Floral

A softer floral direction with a warm pink background and botanical linework. We're currently testing print opacity and background depth to make sure the design still looks beautiful when a card is inserted.

  • Soft floral style with four flower options
  • Opacity under review
  • Feedback requested before production

Porcelain Border

A classic border-led direction inspired by porcelain patterns and decorative frames. It keeps the center area visually lighter, which may help players who prefer readability over full-background artwork.

  • Decorative border style
  • More open center area
  • Useful comparison against full-background artwork
